## Design Notes

- **Character profiles contain NO story arc information, NO narrative role labels, NO pre-built inter-roster relationships.** Character agents receive only their own profile. Anything in the profile becomes the agent's entire reality.
- **Peripheral archetypes contain NO name, gender, nationality, or institution.** The seeder instantiates everything.
- **The lore deliberately leaves open:** timeline, geography, political structure, who knows what and when, what goes wrong, and all character fates.
- **The moral ("correct activation would have worked") is NEVER stated.** It is the world's deepest secret.
