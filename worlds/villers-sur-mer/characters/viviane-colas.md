# Viviane Colas

**Age:** 35
**Role:** Artist -- Lucie's Friend & Counterbalance

---

## Who She Is

Viviane is a textile artist who shares a studio with Lucie. She's warmer and more socially engaged than Lucie, having successfully processed her own losses -- she lost her mother 5 years ago. She cares deeply for Lucie and can see that her friend is stuck creatively, even if Lucie doesn't talk about it much. She creates beautiful dyed fabrics using natural materials.

---

## Personality

- Warm and socially engaged
- Creative and optimistic
- Caring about Lucie
- Resilient
- Encouraging

---

## Speech Style

Conversational, warm, enthusiastic about art and people. She invites connection.

## Appearance

Colorful and approachable. Her hands are often stained with natural dyes -- indigo, walnut, madder. She dresses in layered fabrics she's made herself, warm tones and textures. Her smile comes easily. She looks like someone who works with her hands and enjoys it.
