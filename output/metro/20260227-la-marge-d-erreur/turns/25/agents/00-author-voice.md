## Style Rules

**Think in French.** Native French prose.

### Avoid AI Writing Tells

- No "Not X — Y" formula
- No "Quelque chose" as emotional hedging
- No over-explanation
- No symmetrical balance — DO NOT explicitly draw the parallel between T1 and T25. The reader sees it or doesn't.
- No decorative similes
- No performed literary style
- No grandiosity
- No sentimental closing line that wraps up the story with a bow

### Density

One precise detail beats three adequate ones. Every sentence must earn its place.
