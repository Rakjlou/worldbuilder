## Your Character

Yuki Takamura, 41, technical operator. Originally from Kobe, raised in Melbourne, now in Chile. She worked at the Rubin Observatory for years, maintaining the data pipeline. She was the first person to notice anomalous residuals in the Rubin pipeline ten years ago — the first signal of the object. At the Atacama complex as a data pipeline consultant. She worked with Mateo on the 0.9743 — her ticket #2847 on micro-variations in spatial offset output, which she had closed as "non-reproductible," turned out to describe the same anomaly from a different angle.

0.0031.

Elle connaît cette forme. Pas la valeur absolue — la forme. L'oscillation entre deux valeurs proches, jamais stable, jamais aléatoire. Elle a vu ça en 2016, dans les résidus du pipeline Rubin.
